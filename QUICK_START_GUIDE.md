## 🚀 How to Use the App

### 1. **Report a Lost Item**
   - Click "Add Item" in navigation
   - Select "Lost" as item type
   - Add images (paste image URLs and click "Add" button)
   - Fill in:
     - Title (what was lost)
     - Description (specific details)
     - Category (Electronics, Documents, etc.)
     - Location (where on campus)
     - Date lost
   - Click "Add Post"
   - ✅ Item appears immediately with "Pending" status

### 2. **Report Finding an Item**
   - Click "Add Item"
   - Select "Found"
   - Add photos of the item
   - Fill in details and where you found it
   - Submit
   - ✅ Owner will be notified

### 3. **Search for Items**
   - Go to "Lost & Found Items"
   - **Quick Search**: Type in search box (searches title, location, category)
   - **Quick Filters**: Click "🔴 Lost", "🟢 Found", or "✓ Recovered" buttons
   - **Advanced Filters**: Click "Advanced Filters" button to:
     - Filter by Category
     - Filter by Location
     - Filter by Status (Verified/Pending)
     - Filter by Date Range

### 4. **View Item Details**
   - Click "View Details" on any item card
   - 📸 Browse images using arrow buttons or dots
   - 📖 Read full description, category, location
   - 📞 See owner's contact information
   - ✅ Claim the item if you found it or it's yours

### 5. **Claim or Report Recovery**
   - On item details page, click:
     - "This is Mine!" (if you lost it and found it)
     - "Found This!" (if you found someone's item)
   - Enter:
     - Where you recovered it
     - When you recovered it
   - Click "Submit"
   - ✅ Item marked as recovered, owner notified

### 6. **Manage Your Items**
   - Click "My Items" in navigation
   - See all items you've posted
   - Click "Edit" to update information
   - Click "Delete" to remove (with confirmation)
   - See status of each item (Active/Claimed/Recovered)

## 💡 Tips for Success

### When Reporting Lost Items:
✓ Include specific details only owner would know
✓ Upload clear, well-lit photos
✓ Be specific about location (e.g., "Library 2nd floor" not just "Library")
✓ Post immediately - time matters!

### When Reporting Found Items:
✓ Add photos clearly showing the item
✓ Be specific about where you found it
✓ Include any identifying marks or serial numbers
✓ Check regularly for owner contact attempts

### When Searching:
✓ Use multiple filter combinations
✓ Check "Pending" items too (not verified yet)
✓ Search by location first (narrower results)
✓ Use date range if you remember when you lost it

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Can I upload images directly?**
A: Currently, paste image URLs from the internet. Direct uploads coming soon!

**Q: How long are items listed?**
A: Until they're marked as recovered or you delete them.

**Q: Will I be notified if someone claims my item?**
A: Yes! If your email is in your profile, you'll be notified.

**Q: Can I update my item information?**
A: Yes! Go to "My Items", click "Edit", update details.

**Q: What's "Verified" status?**
A: Items checked by campus security office - more trustworthy!

**Q: How do I know if an item is mine?**
A: Look for specific details that only you would know!

## ⚡ Pro Tips

💡 **Faster Search**: Use specific terms instead of general ones
- ❌ Don't: "lost something"
- ✅ Do: "Lost Samsung Galaxy Watch"

💡 **Better Descriptions**: Include unique details
- ✅ Include: Brand, model, color, condition, any damage
- ✅ Mention: Where exactly you lost it
- ✅ Note: Sentimental value or unique marks

💡 **Quick Recovery**: Check regularly
- ✅ Set reminder to check daily
- ✅ Follow up on potential matches immediately
- ✅ Join campus Lost & Found group chats

💡 **Safety First**: When meeting to exchange
- ✅ Meet in public, busy campus locations
- ✅ Bring ID for verification if item requires it
- ✅ Check item thoroughly before accepting
